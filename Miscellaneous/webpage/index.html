NAU ENERGY DASHBOARD


The Greenest University in the United States, according to the Sustainability Tracking, Assessment & Rating System (STARS) is Colorado State University - Fort Collins.
With a STARS rating of 85.29, they are the first to reach platinum status. NAU trails by 20 points with a Gold status of 65.42, which is pretty good, but we can do better.
With a proper tool for analyzing consumption, monitoring needs, and predicting future scenarios, NAU can set the standard for sustainability.
The NAU Energy Dashboard is the key to promoting sustainability, reducing costs, facilitating research, and ensuring conservation.
